ELBOW

ELBOW r1, alpha, r2

A segmented elbow in the x-z plane. The radius of the arc is r1, the angle is alpha and the radius of the tube segment is r2. The alpha value is in degrees. 

r1 > r2

Example: 

ROTY 90
ELBOW 2.5, 180, 1
ADDZ -4
CYLIND 4, 1
ROTZ -90
MULZ -1
ELBOW 5, 180, 1
DEL 1
ADDX 10
CYLIND 4, 1
ADDZ 4
ROTZ 90
ELBOW 2.5, 180, 1
