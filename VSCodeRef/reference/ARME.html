ARME

ARME l, r1, r2, h, d

A piece of tube starting from an ellipsoid in the y-z plane; parameters according to the figure (penetration lines are also calculated and drawn). 

r1 >= r2+d
l >= h*sqrt(1-(r2-d)2/r12)

Example: 

ELLIPS 3,4
FOR i=1 TO 6
    ARME 6,4,0.5,3,3.7-0.2*i
    ROTZ 30
NEXT i
