REPEAT - UNTIL

REPEAT [statement1
    statement2
    ...
    statementn]
UNTIL condition

The statements between the keywords are executed until the condition becomes true.

The condition is checked after each execution of the statements.

Example: The following four sequences of GDL commands are equivalent

! 1st
FOR i = 1 TO 5 STEP 1
    BRICK 0.5, 0.5, 0.1
    ADDZ 0.3
NEXT i

! 2nd
i = 1
DO
    BRICK 0.5, 0.5, 0.1
    ADDZ 0.3
    i = i + 1
WHILE i <= 5

! 3rd
i = 1
WHILE i <= 5 DO
    BRICK 0.5, 0.5, 0.1
    ADDZ 0.3
    i = i + 1
ENDWHILE

! 4th
i = 1
REPEAT
    BRICK 0.5, 0.5, 0.1
    ADDZ 0.3
    i = i + 1
UNTIL i > 5
